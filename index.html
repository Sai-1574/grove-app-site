Treeak

A habit tracker that grows with you. Build daily habits, tend your living tree, reflect with the journal.

What it does

Treeak turns the slow, invisible work of building habits into something you can see. Each habit you keep up nourishes a tree on your home screen. Skip too many days and it wilts. Stay consistent and it thrives.

Living tree

An animated tree reflects your habit health across five stages — Thriving, Fading, Wilting, Deteriorating, Dead.

Daily, multi-count, and weekly habits

Simple toggles for daily habits. Counters for "drink 3 glasses". Weekly targets for "exercise 4 days a week".

Journal with photos & voice notes

Log how you feel, attach a photo or a quick voice note. Every entry is auto-tagged with your tree's health that day.

Streak insurance

Travelling, sick, or just had a rough day? Mark a day skipped so your tree pauses instead of withering.

Insights

See your best days, peak hours, longest streaks, and which habits influence each other most.

Private by default

Everything stays on your device. Optional Google Drive backup uses a hidden app folder no one else can see — not even Treeak can read your other Drive files.
