Fall off and it begins to fade through five visible stages of health, all the way to dormant. Underneath the metaphor sits a real productivity app. Track daily, weekly, or count-based habits. Reflect in the journal with mood, text, photos, and voice notes. Open the Insights tab for streaks, heatmaps, peak hours, category breakdowns, and weekly reports. Pause habits while you travel. Insure a streak with a planned skip. Everything lives on your device. There are no accounts, no servers, no analytics, and no advertising.
